The Experience

Midnight Oud is not merely a fragrance; it is a nocturnal ritual. It begins with the sharpness of Bergamot, quickly softened by the exotic warmth of Saffron—a brief flicker of light before the descent.

As the heart reveals itself, the romanticism of Damask Rose intertwines with the primal depth of Agarwood (Oud). It is a complex, brooding dialogue that lingers on the skin, evolving as the hours pass.

The dry-down is where the mystery solidifies. Sandalwood and Golden Amber create a creamy, balsamic finish that anchors the scent, leaving a trail that is both haunting and undeniably sophisticated.
